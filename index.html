Our Mission

We are dedicated to safeguarding student rights and advancing human rights across Imo State with passion and precision.

Established under Section 30 of the LAWSAN constitution, our directorate works tirelessly to ensure that the voices of students are heard and their rights protected across all educational institutions in Imo State.
