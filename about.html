About Us

Discover our mission, history, and the team behind our advocacy efforts.

Our Mission

We are committed to protecting and promoting the rights of all students in Imo State, ensuring their voices are heard and their rights upheld across all educational institutions.

Established under Section 30 of the LAWSAN constitution, our directorate serves as a beacon of hope and justice, advocating for human rights and student welfare with unwavering dedication.

Our History

The Imo State Directorate of Students' Rights was founded as part of the Law Students' Association of Nigeria (LAWSAN) to address the growing need for student advocacy and human rights protection in the region.

Under the leadership of Her Excellency Blessing Adeogo Ayakanmi GCOL, the current LAWSAN government appointed a dedicated team to lead this directorate, ensuring that students' rights are not only protected but also promoted through innovative programs and initiatives.
